5-Week Programme

Advanced AI for L&D Professionals

Develop expertise in advanced AI applications for L&D processes and workflows. Move beyond foundational prompts to become a strategic architect of intelligent, scalable, and personalised learning systems.

Delivery

5 weeks total (8 hours). Alternating 2-hour instruction and 1-hour coaching sessions.

Audience

L&D, HR, and OD professionals, instructional designers, and training managers.

Prerequisite

Designed for professionals who regularly use AI to design, develop, and implement learning.

Elevate your L&D capability

This platform-agnostic course is vital for professionals ready to automate complex workflows and ensure the ethical governance of AI. You will learn to directly increase your efficiency and the measurable impact of your L&D function.

1

Strategic & Ethical Integration

Apply an AI framework that includes essential ethical considerations and governance practices, preparing you to lead AI implementation responsibly.

2

Practical Workflow Application

Optimise your instructional design workflows by leveraging the 'AI Sandwich' to rapidly build high-value learning assets, interactive dashboards, and custom AI agents.

3

Learning at Scale

Incorporate learning at scale by designing AI-adaptive learning environments, building internal champion networks, and exploring diverse learning ecosystems.

Weeks 1 & 2: AI Strategy, Prompts and Coaching

Set the strategic baseline for AI capability and get tailored support for your organisational challenges.

Week 1 (2 Hours)

AI Capability, Strategy and Frameworks

  • • Examine AI capability against your organisation's existing policies, strategies, and governance frameworks.
  • • Define the strategic role of L&D in supporting and driving an enterprise-wide AI uplift.
  • • Understand AI capability skills and how they align with the AITD Capability Framework.
  • • The Juggernaut Challenge: Identify a real-world L&D problem to solve across the duration of the course.
Interactive Session Week 2 (1 Hour)

Coaching – 'Ask me anything'

  • • Bring your specific workflow and strategy challenges to a live, open-forum coaching session.
  • • Troubleshoot early prompt designs and discuss aligning them with your frameworks.
  • • Share platform-specific queries (Copilot, ChatGPT, or Gemini).

Weeks 3 & 4: AI Workflows, ID and Coaching

Learn to streamline your instructional design process and build practical, high-value assets using a variety of AI tools.

Week 3 (2 Hours)

Creating Assets and Value Drops

  • • The AI Sandwich: Integrate AI seamlessly into your instructional design workflows to accelerate development and improve output quality.
  • • Asset Creation: Use a variety of Gen AI tools to rapidly produce high-value learning drops, including quick reference guides, job aids, and multimedia content.
  • • Interactive Tools & Agents: Apply basic HTML and advanced prompting to build functional dashboards and deploy custom AI agents.
Interactive Session Week 4 (1 Hour)

Coaching – 'Ask me anything'

  • • Dedicated time to refine the assets and agents you built in Week 3.
  • • Peer review and expert feedback on your instructional design workflows.
  • • Overcome technical hurdles encountered while developing HTML dashboards or value drops.

Week 5: Learning at Scale and Adaptive Environments

Shift your focus to enterprise-wide impact, champion networks, and building sustainable learning ecosystems.

Week 5 (2 Hours)

Ecosystems and Champion Networks

  • Learning at Scale: Discover strategies for incorporating AI to support wide-scale learning initiatives across the organisation.
  • Champion Networks: Learn how to identify, build, and nurture internal champion networks to drive AI adoption and provide peer support.
  • Adaptive Ecosystems: Foster lifelong learning through supportive, AI-adaptive environments and explore different learning ecosystem models tailored to your business.
Final Presentation Week 5 (Showcase)

The Juggernaut Challenge

  • • Present your practical, AI-enabled workflow, agent, or learning asset prototype to the cohort.
  • • Detail how your solution scales within your broader learning ecosystem.
  • • Receive constructive peer and expert feedback to refine your implementation strategy.

The Juggernaut Challenge

Course-long Project

Rather than completing separate, disjointed tasks each week, you will develop one continuous, workplace-based project across the 5 weeks. This ensures you leave with a highly relevant, practical, and ready-to-implement AI solution.

Wks 1-2

Define the Challenge

Identify a specific L&D bottleneck or strategic opportunity in your organisation that can be solved using advanced AI capabilities.

Wks 3-4

Build the Solution

Apply your new workflow skills, the 'AI Sandwich', and interactive tools to prototype your custom AI agent, dashboard, or learning asset.

Wk 5

Present & Scale

Showcase your solution to the cohort, demonstrate how it fits into your learning ecosystem, and gather peer feedback for final refinement.
